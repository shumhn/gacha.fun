# VOIDDECK Privacy Notice

Last updated: July 11, 2026

VOIDDECK is a Devnet-only collectible demonstration. It does not create user accounts and does not operate an application analytics or advertising backend.

## Data used by the app

- The wallet selected through Mobile Wallet Adapter provides a public Solana address to the app.
- Pulls, collectible ownership, and inventory updates are written to public Solana Devnet accounts and transactions.
- The app stores limited interface and inventory state locally on the device.
- External wallet private keys and seed phrases are never requested or received; signing is performed by the connected wallet.
- The optional built-in Devnet test wallet stores a generated test key locally on the device. It is only for test SOL and must never receive real assets or funds.

## Third-party infrastructure

The app communicates with Solana Devnet, MagicBlock Devnet RPC services, and the wallet chosen by the user. Those services may process network metadata under their own terms.

## Deletion

Local app data, including the built-in Devnet test key, can be deleted by clearing VOIDDECK's storage or uninstalling the app. Public blockchain records cannot be deleted because of the nature of the network.
