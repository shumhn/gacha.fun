# Brand - VOIDDECK

Status: active

VOIDDECK is a premium sci-fi creature card universe. The app uses a matte collector-terminal shell, crisp off-white type, signal green only for verified state, and rarity colors only where they carry meaning. The first set is Genesis Signal.

- Background: `#0B0D0C`
- Surface: `#151816`
- Raised surface: `#1D211E`
- Text: `#F4F7F2`
- Muted text: `#9BA49D`
- Verified: `#7DE2A2`
- Common: `#8FA08C`
- Rare: `#55CBE8`
- Epic: `#F0B85A`
- Legendary: `#F2D16B`

Typography uses the platform sans family with compact, readable hierarchy. Voice is mysterious, precise, and never casino-like. Product language says open, reveal, card, vault, and proof; never capsule, spin, jackpot, or win.
